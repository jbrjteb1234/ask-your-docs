Harper's Bike Hire — Booking, Cancellation and Refund Policy (2026)

BOOKINGS
Bookings can be made online, by phone, or in the shop. Full payment is taken
at the time of booking. A booking is only confirmed once you receive the
confirmation email with your hire reference.

CANCELLATIONS BY YOU
- 48 hours' notice or more before pick-up: full refund, no questions asked.
- Less than 48 hours but more than 24 hours: 50% refund.
- Less than 24 hours' notice, or no-show: no refund.
Refunds go back to the card used to pay, within 5 working days.

CANCELLATIONS BY US
If we cancel (for example, severe weather warnings or mechanical shortage),
you choose either a full refund or a free rebooking on any date within 12
months. If we cancel with less than 24 hours' notice we also add a £10
voucher per bike.

LATE RETURNS
Bikes are due back by the time on your hire agreement. Returns up to 1 hour
late are charged £5 per bike. After 1 hour, a further half-day rate applies.
Bikes kept overnight without arrangement are charged a full extra day plus
£20 administration fee.

DAMAGE AND LOSS
Normal wear (tyres, brake pads) is on us. Damage beyond normal wear is
charged at cost from the deposit; anything above the deposit is invoiced.
A lost or stolen bike is charged at replacement value: up to £400 for
standard bikes, up to £1,800 for e-bikes, less the deposit already held.
Lost keys or locks are £15. Lost helmets are £20.

WEATHER
We hire in all weather. Rain does not qualify for a refund, but if the Met
Office issues an amber or red warning for the hire period we treat it as a
cancellation by us (see above).
