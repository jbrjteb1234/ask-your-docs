All prices include a helmet, lock and puncture repair kit. VAT included.

|------|---------------|----------|------|

| Hybrid | £15 | £22 | £90 |

## Electric bikes

| Bike | Half day (4h) | Full day | Week |
|------|---------------|----------|------|
| E-city | £25 | £38 | £150 |
| E-mountain | £30 | £45 | £180 |

E-bike batteries last 60–80 km per charge. A spare battery can be added to
any e-bike hire for £8 per day.

## Extras

- Child seat: £5 per day
- Trailer (up to 2 children): £10 per day
- Pannier set: £4 per day
- GPS computer: £6 per day

## Deposits

Every hire requires a refundable deposit: £50 for standard bikes, £100 for
e-bikes, taken on the card used to book and released within 3 working days
of the bike coming back undamaged.

## Group discounts

Groups of 6 or more bikes booked together get 15% off the total. Schools and
registered charities get 20% off midweek hires (Monday–Thursday).
