# DEMO.md — Loom script (2–3 minutes)

**Recorded 2026-07-12:** https://www.loom.com/share/aae3b8fce505454182f2319cc2bee1f9

Goal: show a prospect that the assistant answers from *their* content, cites
sources, and refuses to guess — then reveal the unanswered-questions log as
their content roadmap. Record at 1280×720. Have the deployed URL open on the
`/demo` page and `/admin` in a second tab (already logged in).

Fictional business used here: **Harper's Bike Hire** (samples/ are its price
list, policies, and FAQ). For a real prospect call, ingest THEIR site first
and demo on their own content — that is the whole sales weapon.

---

## Beat 1 — the hook (0:00–0:20)
- On the `/demo` page. "This is an ordinary web page. The only change is one
  line of code — this script tag." Briefly show the tag at the bottom of the
  page source.
- "The chat bubble is the whole integration. Let's ask it about the business."

## Beat 2 — the answerable question (0:20–0:55)
- Open the widget. Ask: **"How much is it to hire an e-mountain bike for a
  full day?"**
- Answer comes back: **£45**, with the source shown underneath.
- Say it plainly: "It didn't just answer — it told you *where* the answer came
  from. Your customers can trust it, and so can you."

## Beat 3 — the nuanced question (0:55–1:30)
- Ask: **"I need to cancel two days before pick-up — do I get a refund?"**
- It applies the 48-hour rule and quotes the policy, cited.
- "This isn't keyword search. It read the policy and answered the actual
  question — still only from the documents."

## Beat 4 — the honest failure (1:30–2:05)
- Ask something the docs don't cover: **"Do you sell gift vouchers?"**
- It replies "I don't know" and offers the human contact.
- Land the point: "This is the important one. A generic chatbot would make
  something up. This says *I don't know* and points to a human — because every
  answer has to come from your documents."

## Beat 5 — the admin roadmap (2:05–2:45)
- Switch to `/admin`. Show the **unanswered-questions** list.
- "Every question it couldn't answer is logged here. That's not a failure —
  it's your content roadmap: the exact gaps to fill. Keeping this list short
  is what the monthly retainer does."
- Show the recent-questions list with sources per answer.

## Close (2:45–3:00)
- "One script tag, answers from your own content with receipts, and a list of
  what to improve. I can load your site into this before our call — just send
  me the link."
